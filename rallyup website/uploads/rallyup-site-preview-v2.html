how it works

three steps. no ghostwriter drama.

1

we map your voice.

a 45-minute interview + your last 12 months of posts, docs, and talks. we build a living voice profile — how you open, how you argue, which phrases are yours, which topics you'll never touch. it's the foundation everything else runs on.

2

we draft in your voice.

our system writes 2–5 posts a week pulled from your actual point of view. a human editor reviews every post against what's performing on linkedin this week. no chatgpt defaults. no generic "thought leadership."

3

you approve in slack.

posts land in your slack dm. thumbs up, thumbs down, or request an edit. 30 seconds. we schedule, post, and track performance. you stay focused on the business.

faq

questions we get a lot.

how much does this cost?
engagements start at $999/month per exec, scaling up based on volume and team size. we'll scope your rollout on the call. no surprises, no contracts you can't get out of.
how much of my time does this actually take?
45 min to set up your voice profile. after that, 30 seconds per post to approve in slack. that's it. you're not writing, editing, or scheduling.
does it actually sound like me, or is this just chatgpt with better packaging?
no. our system is trained on your voice, and a human editor reviews every post before it hits your slack. if you wouldn't say it, we don't send it. you get veto power on every single post.
what if i've never posted on linkedin before?
that's most of our clients. we handle positioning, topics, cadence, and what the algorithm's rewarding this week. you just approve posts. consistent posting is how you build the habit — we just make consistent possible.
can i run this for one exec, or does it have to be a whole team?
either. solo exec is our most common start. a lot of ceos bring us in for themselves, see the result, then roll it out to their full leadership team.
how do you measure results?
we track everything — impressions, engagement rate, profile views, inbound dms, connection requests from icp. you get a monthly report. most clients see 5–10x their baseline impressions within 30 days.
what's the commitment, and what if it doesn't work?
3-month minimum. if we haven't meaningfully grown your exec's profile by day 90, month 4 is on us. linkedin isn't a one-post strategy — the algorithm and your audience both need time to learn your voice.
